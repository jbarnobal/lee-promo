HOW TO JOIN?

  1. Open to all residents of the Philippines nationwide who are at least 18-35 years old and above.
  2. Participants may join by following our page Lee Jeans Philippines on Facebook and @leejeansphils on Instagram and choose which challenge from campaign images or create your own photo/video.
  3. Make sure you are wearing any Lee Apparel on your entry
  4. Share your entries in Photo or Video form then post it on both your Facebook and Instagram accounts.
  5. Tag three (3) of your friends on the caption challenging them to join the Move Your Lee Challenge.
  6. Don’t forget to tag @leejeansphils on your entries on Facebook and Instagram and use the hashtags #MoveYourLeeChallenge, #StrechYourImagination, #LeeJeansPH,
  7. Each participant can send as many entries as they want
  8. Cash Prize is non - transferable
  9. Promo runs from November 5 – December 7, 2018
  10. Deadline of submission of entries is on December 7, 2018 - 12midnight
